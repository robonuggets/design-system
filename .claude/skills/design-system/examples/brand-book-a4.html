Typography

Outfit. Clean.
Built to read.

Outfit is the system font — weights 300 to 800. Body sits at 400, headings at 700/800. Space Mono for uppercase labels and codes.

Space Mono · Labels · Codes · Timestamps
Principles
  • Plain language — no jargon in UI or diagrams.
  • No white backgrounds. Dark surfaces default.
  • No vignettes on images. No emojis in UI.
  • SVGs over emojis. Icons are inline, crisp.
  • Cinematic, not catalog — one subject at a time.
  • Orange dominates. Grey supports. Green means success.
