Typography

Outfit. Clean.
Built to read.

Outfit is the system font — weights 300 to 800. Body copy sits at 400, headings at 700/800. Never Inter. Space Mono handles uppercase labels, codes, and timestamps.

Space Mono · Labels · Codes · Timestamps
Light 300 — supporting Regular 400 — body Medium 500 — emphasis Semibold 600 — UI labels Bold 700 — headings Black 800 — display
Principles
  • Plain language — no jargon in UI or diagrams.
  • No white backgrounds. Dark surfaces default.
  • No vignettes. No emojis in UI.
  • SVGs over emojis. Icons inline, crisp.
  • Cinematic, not catalog — one subject, not grids of items.
  • Orange dominates. Grey supports. Green means success.
